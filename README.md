# Adidas Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Adidas sales data. It provides insights into overall business performance, sales trends, regional distribution, product performance, and retailer effectiveness to support data-driven decision making.

## Business Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profitability
* Identify high and low performing regions and states
* Understand product demand and performance
* Evaluate retailer contributions
* Support pricing and margin analysis

## Dataset

The dataset contains approximately 9,600 rows of retailer-level sales data for the years 2020 and 2021.

Key fields include:

* Retailer, Retailer ID
* Invoice Date
* Region, State, City
* Product
* Price per Unit
* Units Sold
* Total Sales
* Operating Profit
* Operating Margin
* Sales Method

The data is clean and directly used for analysis.

## Dashboard Components

### KPI Summary

* Total Sales
* Operating Profit
* Units Sold
* Average Price per Unit
* Operating Margin

### Sales Trend Analysis

Total sales by month using an area chart to identify seasonal patterns.

Insight:
Sales peak in July, August, and December, while February and March show lower performance.

### Geographic Analysis

Sales by state using a map visualization.

Insight:
Top performing states include California, New York, Texas, and Florida.

### Regional Analysis

Sales by region using a donut chart.

Insight:
The West region contributes the highest share of total sales.

### Product Analysis

Sales by product using a bar chart.

Insight:
Men’s Street Footwear is the top performing product.

### Retailer Analysis

Sales by retailer using a bar chart.

Insight:
West Gear is the leading retailer, while Walmart and Amazon show lower contribution.

### Interactivity

* Region slicer
* Date range filter
* Cross-filtering across visuals
